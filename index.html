About Me

Hello! My name is Hamish Mead, a Year 9 student at Newman Catholic College passionate about making a real difference for young people in our community. My aim is to create spaces where youth feel supported, heard, and where they can just be themseleves.

I’m running for the Cairns Youth Council because I want to advocate for better mental health support, more inclusive community events, and opportunities for every young voice to be heard, not just for today, but every day. Vote #1 for Hamish Mead!

My Vision

  • ✅ Improve youth mental health resources in schools and community spaces.
  • ✅ Help for youth in community spaces and libaries for their studies.
  • ✅ Increase free or low-cost youth activities in the Northen Beaches.
  • ✅ Strengthen the link between young people and the adults.
