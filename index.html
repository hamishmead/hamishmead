About Me

Hi! I'm Hamish Mead, a Year 9 student at Newman Catholic College. I'm passionate about making a real difference for young people in our community.

I'm running for Cairns Youth Council to advocate for better mental health support, inclusive events, and youth representation, not just today, but every day. Vote #1 for Hamish Mead!

My Vision

Mental Health

Raise awareness and support for youth mental health in Cairns. Everyone faces challenges, it's time we talk about it.

Academic Support

Help students struggling with schoolwork by promoting better resources and hands-on guidance in community spaces.

Youth Events

Promote more free or low-cost events in the Northern Beaches that connect youth with community and promote mental wellness.
