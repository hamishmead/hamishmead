About Me

Hello! My name is Hamish Mead, a Year 9 student at Newman Catholic College passionate about making a real difference for young people in our community. My aim is to create spaces where youth feel supported, heard, and where they can just be themseleves.

I’m running for the Cairns Youth Council because I want to advocate for better mental health support, more inclusive community events, and opportunities for every young voice to be heard, not just for today, but every day. Vote #1 for Hamish Mead!

My Vision

  • During the campaign period, I would like to raise awareness about youth mental health in Cairns. Everyone has their own issues that they struggle with everyday. Mental Health is an important thing in Cairns youth, and it is something we need to address.
  • Moreover, many youth struggle with their schoolwork, whether it is that they don't know how to do their work, or something else. I would like to try and raise awareness about this as well.
  • Something that the Northen beaches doesn't have is many youth events. Once I am elected, I would like to make some more free or low-cost events. These events will help improve youth mental health as well as strengthing the link between the Youth and Adults.
